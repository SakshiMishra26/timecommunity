About The time Community bank

The time Community bank (TCB) is a mutual support network of people engaged in reciprocal exchange of services, skills, and goods through a timebank where the currency is an hour of time for everyone.

Through membership in The time Community bank, people:

  • create and strengthen community bonds.
  • create economic freedom by providing an alternative means to get needs and desires met.
  • encourage creativity in redefining self-sufficiency, interdependence, and valuation of time.

Our Paradigm

The time Community bank introduces a paradigm where members reflect on the available resources in their lives moving from a perception of scarcity and lack to abundance and wholeness.

Our Guiding Vision

Our guiding vision is to make social infrastructure visible within our communities. Social infrastructure describes the invisible bonds and relationships which constitute a generative capital that can be accumulated and circulated through timebanking.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote and sustain a robust timebanking infrastructure which supports non-monetary transactions amongst individuals and organizations allowing a greater sum of the realized value of work to be retained and shared within local communities.

Our Focus

We are particularly focused on partnering with organizations which have a broad base of stakeholders who might benefit from deploying timebanking as a method for capturing and compensating work that presently lives in the form of reciprocal goodwill transactions and creating an infrastructure where the time given over to these worthy causes may be traded with other members within the timebank for services those members have offered.

Our Goal

Our goal is to develop and sustain a complementary community currency which decreases financial pressure, reinforces the fabric of trust, and creates new opportunities for engagement between stakeholders within our communities.

Fiscal Sponsorship

The time Community bank is a fiscally sponsored project of Open Collective Foundation.
